Differentiated Technology

Natural Gas Virtual Pipeline

Core has natural gas virtual pipeline and offers turnkey capabilities, 100% modularity & scalability to meet any project demands.

Features

  • More than 360 MCF storage per trailer & 3,700+ PSI operating pressure
  • Fully-automated design with 24/7 service support
  • Composite & light-weight design moduless & remote locations
  • Composite & light-weight design modules
  • Achieve more than 60% fuel substitution, a rates on dual-fuel engines
  • Custom gas delivery specification by temperature & pressure
  • Significantly reduce CO2 & NO2 emissions

Automated Fueling System

CORE next-generation AI-powered fueling system enables shale operators to increase frac stage.

Features

  • Proprietary fuel valves avoid fuel clogging and spills, enhance pump performance and eliminate frac downtime.
  • 26+ custom-built and heavy-duty motorized hose reels with over 450 ft capacity
  • Individual asset metering & reel level IOT sensors equipped with dynamic flow rate modulation
  • 21,500 gal fully self-contained fuel storage tank double wall design, under tank visibility and overfill protection
  • Safe and efficient automated frac fueling

Software data analytics

CORE SaaS platform streamlines the delivery of the industry’s most accurate time-series and operational data directly to your laptop, tablet and smartphone.

Computer-based AI technology captures real-time data related to fuel consumption and substitution rates, analyzes tank fuel levels and flow rate modulation by stage, fleet and asset.

Advanced reporting system allows for immediate, accurate, and on-time compliance of mandatory tax reporting for natural gas & diesel fuel, replacing manual, error-prone processes
